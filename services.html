Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Is the free audit actually free?
Yes — no credit card, no hidden fees, no pitch call forced on you. The free audit delivers a real, scored report based on your listing details. You get genuine value before spending anything.
Do you only work with Hawaii listings?
Not at all. Ksandr serves VR owners globally — any platform, any market, any country. The tool and advisory work is designed for Airbnb, Vrbo, Booking.com, and direct booking across all markets.
What's the difference between Free and Pro?
The free audit gives you your score, quick wins, and a priority action. The Pro Report goes deeper — competitor comparisons, custom keyword recommendations, and platform-specific OTA strategy. Think: overview vs. full playbook.
I only have one listing. Is a retainer worth it?
Usually not right away. Start with an audit and a strategy session. Once you've optimized and started seeing results, retainers make the most sense for owners scaling past 2–3 units or managing high seasonality.
How do you handle confidential business information?
Everything shared in sessions and reports stays between us. Ksandr doesn't share, publish, or reference client details without explicit permission. Any case studies used are anonymized by default.
What if I'm not a vacation rental owner?
The Ksandr AI track is built specifically for small business owners of all types — restaurants, boutiques, service businesses, and more. If you want to use AI smarter in your business, there's a path for you here.
